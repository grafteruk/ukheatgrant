About the Grant

What is the Boiler Upgrade Scheme?

The Boiler Upgrade Scheme (BUS) is a UK Government initiative designed to help homeowners in England and Wales replace fossil fuel heating systems with cleaner, more efficient alternatives.

Eligible homeowners can receive a grant of up to £7,500 towards the cost of installing an air source heat pump — one of the most popular and effective low-carbon heating solutions available.

The grant is applied directly by your installer, meaning you never have to handle the paperwork yourself. You simply pay the reduced amount after the grant is deducted.

Eligibility

Do you qualify?

Most homeowners in England and Wales are eligible. Here's what typically qualifies you for the grant.

✓

You own the property — the grant is not available to renters

✓

✓

You currently use a gas boiler, oil, or electric heating system

✓

Your property has a valid EPC (Energy Performance Certificate)

✓

You have not previously received a BUS grant for the same property

✓

Your loft and cavity walls are insulated where possible
